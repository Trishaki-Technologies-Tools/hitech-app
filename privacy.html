Privacy Policy

Important Notice: Background Location Access

Hitech Pragati collects location data to enable automated attendance tracking, direct sales executive (DSE) route mapping, and real-time task coordinates verification **even when the app is closed or not in use**.

This tracking is a core requirement for the operational needs of your enterprise workspace and coordinates are visible only to authorized Team Leaders (TL) and managers. This location data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hitech Pragati. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our mobile app designed for Direct Sales Executives (DSE) and Team Leaders (TL).

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services effectively, we collect the following types of information:

A. Location Data (Foreground & Background)

  • Background Location: The app monitors location coordinates in the background (when the app is minimized or the screen is locked) to verify check-ins, record field visits, and log attendance during duty hours.
  • Foreground Location: Captured when executing tasks, taking photo updates, or submitting client logs.

B. Personal & Account Information

  • Name, phone number, email address, employee ID, and role.
  • Login credentials generated by the enterprise administrator.

C. Device Information

  • Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier, and network connection status.

D. Media & Files

  • Images/documents explicitly uploaded by you (e.g., invoices, visitor details, client site photos).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following business purposes:

  • To verify employee attendance, check-ins, and task assignments.
  • To optimize direct sales routing and coordinate activities between DSEs and Team Leaders (TL).
  • To ensure transparent and secure operations within the Hitech corporate ecosystem.
  • To secure accounts and prevent unauthorized system login attempts.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with any external third parties. Data is only accessible to:

  • Your Employer/Administrator: Authorized team leads, administrators, and management of your company.
  • Service Providers: Cloud storage and notification services (such as secure databases and MSG91 API helpers) that process data strictly on our behalf.

5. Data Security

We implement strict administrative, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard your personal data, including secure database protocols and secure HTTPS communication channels. All access to location history is restricted to verified management personnel.

6. User Controls and Permissions

You can manage permissions for location tracking, camera, and files directly through your mobile device's settings. However, disabling background location permissions may restrict key features of the application, such as automatic attendance logging and daily dispatch verification.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your company administrator or our support team.
